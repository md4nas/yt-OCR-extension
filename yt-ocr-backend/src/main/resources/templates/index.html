    Frequently Asked Questions

    What image formats are supported?

    We support all common image formats including JPG, PNG,. The maximum file size is 5MB.

    How accurate is the OCR technology?

    Our OCR technology uses Tesseract engine with over 95% accuracy for clear, high-quality images with standard fonts. Accuracy may vary with handwritten text or poor image quality.

    Is my data secure and private?

    Yes! All image processing is done locally on your device. We don't store or transmit your images to external servers, ensuring complete privacy.

    Can I use this for multiple languages?

    Currently, we support English text recognition. We're working on adding support for more languages in future updates.

    How do I use the browser extension?

    After installing the extension, simply click the extension icon and select "Capture Screen" to extract text from any part of your screen or webpage.

    Why does OCR processing take time?

    OCR processing involves multiple steps: capturing your image in real-time, sending it to our backend server for AI analysis, and returning the extracted text. Processing time depends on image size, complexity, and server load. Typically takes 2-5 seconds for optimal results.

    Why is some text cut off or missing?

    If text appears cropped or incomplete, try selecting a larger area around the text you want to capture. Ensure the entire text block is within your selection boundaries and avoid cutting off words at the edges for better accuracy.
